Last updated: April 12, 2025  ·  Effective: April 12, 2025

Summary: BestIPTVTuto is a review blog. We collect limited data to operate the site and may use cookies for analytics and advertising. We never sell your personal data. Read the full policy below.

1. Who We Are

BestIPTVTuto ("we", "us", or "our") is an independent IPTV review blog operating at bestiptvtuto.com. We publish IPTV reviews, setup guides, tips, and comparisons. We are not an IPTV provider and do not sell streaming subscriptions.

For privacy-related questions, contact us at: privacy@bestiptvtuto.com

2. What Data We Collect

Data you provide voluntarily

  • Newsletter subscription: your email address when you sign up for our mailing list.
  • Contact form: your name, email address, and message content if you contact us.
  • Comments: name, email, and comment text if we enable comment functionality.

Data collected automatically

  • Log data: IP address, browser type, referring URL, pages visited, and timestamps — collected by our hosting provider (Cloudflare).
  • Analytics data: page views, session duration, and traffic sources via analytics tools (e.g. Google Analytics or Cloudflare Web Analytics).
  • Cookies: see Section 5 below.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect to:

  • Deliver and improve the content on BestIPTVTuto
  • Send newsletter emails to subscribers who have opted in
  • Respond to enquiries submitted through our contact form
  • Analyse site performance and understand how visitors use the site
  • Detect and prevent spam, fraud, or security incidents
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties.

Some articles on BestIPTVTuto may contain sponsored content or affiliate links. This means we may earn a commission if you click a link and make a purchase, at no extra cost to you. All sponsored posts and affiliate relationships are clearly disclosed at the top of the relevant article.

Sponsorship and affiliate arrangements do not influence our review scores or editorial conclusions. Our opinions are our own.

5. Cookies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies on BestIPTVTuto. These may include:

  • Essential cookies: required for the site to function (e.g. security, session handling).
  • Analytics cookies: help us understand visitor behaviour (e.g. Google Analytics).
  • Advertising cookies: used to serve relevant advertisements or track ad performance if we display ads.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some site functionality.

We use the following third-party services which may collect data according to their own privacy policies:

7. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purpose it was collected:

  • Newsletter email addresses: until you unsubscribe.
  • Contact form submissions: up to 12 months.
  • Server log data: typically 30–90 days as set by Cloudflare.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Correction: ask us to correct inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: request that we delete your data ("right to be forgotten").

  • Portability: receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@bestiptvtuto.com. We will respond within 30 days.

BestIPTVTuto is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has submitted personal data to us, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.
