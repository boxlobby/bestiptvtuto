DMCA Policy

Last updated: April 12, 2025  ·  Digital Millennium Copyright Act compliance

Quick summary: BestIPTVTuto respects intellectual property rights. If you believe your copyrighted work has been used on this site without authorisation, you can submit a DMCA takedown notice using the process below.

1. Overview

BestIPTVTuto ("we", "us", "our") respects the intellectual property rights of others and expects our users and contributors to do the same. In accordance with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act of 1998 (DMCA), we will respond promptly to valid notices of alleged copyright infringement.

This DMCA Policy explains how to submit a takedown notice, how we handle counter-notices, and our repeat infringer policy.

2. What We Publish

BestIPTVTuto is a review and editorial blog. Our content includes:

  • Original written reviews and guides authored by our editorial team
  • Third-party images licensed from stock photography providers (e.g. Unsplash)
  • Screenshots used for illustrative and review purposes under fair use
  • Embedded videos from YouTube and other platforms

We do not host, stream, distribute, or reproduce any IPTV broadcast content, TV channels, films, or series. BestIPTVTuto is strictly an editorial site.

3. How to Submit a DMCA Takedown Notice

If you are a copyright owner, or authorised to act on behalf of one, and you believe that content on BestIPTVTuto infringes your copyright, please send a written DMCA notice to our designated agent.

Your notice must include all of the following:

  1. Identification of the copyrighted work you claim has been infringed — or, if multiple works are covered by a single notification, a representative list of such works.
  2. Identification of the infringing material on our site that you request to be removed, including the full URL(s) of the page(s) where it appears.
  3. Your contact information including your full name, address, telephone number, and email address.
  4. A statement that you have a good faith belief that the use of the material in the manner complained of is not authorised by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law.
  5. A statement that the information in your notice is accurate, and under penalty of perjury, that you are the copyright owner or authorised to act on behalf of the owner.
  6. Your physical or electronic signature.

Send your completed DMCA notice to:

dmca@bestiptvtuto.com

4. Our Response to Valid Notices

Upon receipt of a valid DMCA notice, we will:

  • Acknowledge receipt of your notice within 5 business days
  • Remove or disable access to the allegedly infringing content promptly
  • Notify the person who posted the content (if applicable) that it has been removed
  • Keep a record of the notice in accordance with the DMCA

Please note that we are not required to act on notices that are incomplete or do not meet the requirements of 17 U.S.C. § 512(c)(3).

5. Counter-Notice Procedure

If you believe content was removed from BestIPTVTuto as a result of a mistake or misidentification, you may submit a counter-notice. Your counter-notice must include:

  • Your full name, address, phone number, and email address
  • Identification of the material that was removed and the location where it appeared before removal
  • A statement under penalty of perjury that you have a good faith belief that the material was removed due to mistake or misidentification
  • A statement that you consent to the jurisdiction of the federal district court for your judicial district, and that you will accept service of process from the party who filed the original notice
  • Your physical or electronic signature

Send counter-notices to: dmca@bestiptvtuto.com

Upon receipt of a valid counter-notice, we will forward it to the original complainant. If the complainant does not notify us within 10–14 business days that they have filed a court action, we may restore the removed content at our discretion.

6. Repeat Infringer Policy

BestIPTVTuto maintains a strict policy against repeat copyright infringers. Users or contributors who are subject to multiple valid DMCA notices will have their access to contribute content permanently terminated.

7. Misuse of DMCA Notices

Submitting a false DMCA notice is a serious matter. Under 17 U.S.C. § 512(f), any person who knowingly and materially misrepresents that content is infringing may be liable for damages, including costs and legal fees incurred by the accused infringer, the service provider, or the copyright owner.

We reserve the right to seek damages from any person who submits a bad-faith or fraudulent DMCA notice.

8. Fair Use

BestIPTVTuto publishes content for purposes of commentary, criticism, review, and education. Some use of copyrighted material on this site may be protected by the doctrine of fair use under 17 U.S.C. § 107, or equivalent provisions in other jurisdictions. We encourage copyright holders to consider fair use before submitting a takedown notice.
