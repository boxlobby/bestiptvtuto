### Troubleshooting Common Issues

During setup, you might encounter issues. Here are some common problems and possible solutions:

1.  **"No Channels Found":** Double-check that the M3U URL is correct and accessible. Restart your Plex Media Server. Some providers block certain IP addresses; using a VPN might help.

2.  **Incorrect Channel Mapping:** Manually adjust the channel mappings in the Plex settings. Review the channel names and numbers to ensure they match your provider's lineup.

3.  **Buffering Issues:** Ensure your Plex server has sufficient processing power. Try reducing the streaming quality in the Plex client settings. Check your internet connection speed.

4.  **EPG Not Loading:** Verify that the EPG XML URL is correct. Try refreshing the EPG data in the Plex settings. The EPG file may be temporarily unavailable from your provider.
