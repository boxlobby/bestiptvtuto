### Troubleshooting Screen Mirroring

*   **Roku Not Appearing?** Ensure both your Roku and the mirroring device are connected to the same Wi-Fi network. Restart both devices.
*   **Laggy Playback?** Screen mirroring relies heavily on a strong Wi-Fi connection. Move your Roku and mirroring device closer to your router, or consider upgrading your Wi-Fi network. Close unnecessary apps on your mirroring device to free up resources.
*   **Sound Issues?** Check the volume settings on both your mirroring device and your TV. Ensure the correct audio output is selected on your TV.
