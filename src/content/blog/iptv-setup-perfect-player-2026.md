## Troubleshooting Common Issues (2026)

Even with a straightforward setup, you might encounter issues. Here are some common problems and potential solutions:

1.  **"No Channels Available" or "Playlist Loading Error":** Double-check the M3U URL for typos. Ensure your IPTV service is active and that the URL hasn't expired. Restart Perfect Player and your device.
2.  **Buffering or Lagging:** This could be due to a slow internet connection, an overloaded IPTV server, or incorrect buffer settings. Try reducing the video quality in Perfect Player's settings. Experiment with different decoder settings. Consider upgrading your internet plan.
3.  **EPG Not Loading:** Verify the EPG URL. Ensure the EPG source is compatible with Perfect Player. Check the update interval in the EPG settings.
4. **Audio Issues:** Check your device's volume settings and Perfect Player's audio settings. Try switching between different audio tracks if available.
5. **Perfect Player Crashing:** Clear the app's cache. Reinstall Perfect Player. Ensure your device meets the minimum system requirements.
