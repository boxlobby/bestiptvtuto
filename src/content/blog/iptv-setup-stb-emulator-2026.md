### Troubleshooting Common Issues

If you encounter issues during the setup process, here are some common troubleshooting tips:

1.  **Verify Portal URL:** Double-check that you have entered the Portal URL correctly. Even a minor typo can prevent the emulator from connecting to the server.

2.  **Check MAC Address:** Ensure that the MAC address is entered correctly and that it matches the MAC address registered with your IPTV provider.

3.  **Internet Connection:** Verify that your device has a stable internet connection. Try accessing other websites or applications to confirm your internet is working correctly.

4.  **Firewall Issues:** Some firewalls or network configurations might block the STB Emulator from connecting to the IPTV server. Ensure that your firewall is not blocking the application.

5.  **Contact Your Provider:** If you've tried all the troubleshooting steps and are still unable to connect, contact your IPTV service provider for assistance. They can help you verify your account details and troubleshoot any server-side issues.
