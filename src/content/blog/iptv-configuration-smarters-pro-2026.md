## Step-by-Step Smarters Pro Configuration

Configuring Smarters Pro for IPTV streaming is a straightforward process. Follow these steps to get started.

### Setting Up Your Connection

1.  **Download and Install:** Download the latest version of Smarters Pro from the official app store for your device (Android, iOS, Smart TV, etc.). Ensure your device meets the minimum system requirements listed. Then, install the application.

2.  **Launch the Application:** Launch the Smarters Pro application. You'll be presented with different login options. Select the option that corresponds to the information provided by your IPTV service provider. Options include:
    *   **Login with Xtream Codes API:** Select this if your provider uses Xtream Codes.
    *   **Load Your Playlist or File/URL:** Choose this if you have an M3U URL or a local M3U file.
    *   **Login with M3U URL:** Allows direct input of the URL.

3.  **Enter Your Credentials:** Carefully enter the required information based on the chosen login method.
    *   **Xtream Codes API:** Enter your username, password, and the server URL provided by your IPTV provider. Double-check for typos.
    *   **M3U URL/File:** Enter the full URL of your M3U playlist or locate the M3U file on your device using the file browser.

4.  **Save and Login:** After entering your credentials or selecting your M3U file/URL, tap the "Add User" or "Login" button. The app will attempt to connect.
